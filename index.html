Help

  1. Enter a valid email address.
  2. Enter password
  3. Click Sign up.

  1. After successful account creation, you are prompted to select the type of user to are i.e. Doctor or Patient.
  2. Select the appropriate button, labeled as either patient or doctor.
  3. Fill in all the fields
  4. Select the profile image slot to upload an image (optional).
  5. To save your details, press save and proceed.
  6. Not filling your personal information hinders you from proceeding

  1. All doctors are displayed in a list, displaying their available times, names and specialization.
  2. To choose get to see a doctor, click one from the list displayed.
  3. You can also search for the doctor based on their specialization.

  1. After choosing a doctor, you need to input your medical details ie symptoms and medical conditions.
  2. Your symptoms are generally how you feel to help the doctor diagnose you better eg vomiting etc.
  3. To choose a symptom, press the buttons on the screen, you can select as many as needed.
  4. Your special conditions are any medical conditions a doctor needs to be aware of before administering treatment e.g. Diabetes.
  5. To input this, type it on the space provided below the symptom’s buttons. Be as brief as possible.
  6. To save this information and proceed to the next step, press the next button at the bottom of the screen.

  1. After filling your symptoms, you are prompted to pay for the service you are about to receive from the doctor.
  2. Carefully, read the payment instructions before proceeding to press pay.
  3. To pay, Press the button labelled pay via mpesa.
  4. A screen prompting you to input your MPESA pin appears, input your pin and press okay.
  5. Wait for an MPESA confirmation message before clicking proceed, this may take up to 10 seconds.
  6. Click the proceed button, to be able to move to the next screen.
  7. Not paying, hinders you from proceeding to the next step.

  1. After successful payment, a new list of the doctor(s) your requested for appears.
  2. The list is sorted in the order of the most recent request, and the name of the doctor.
  3. Click on your doctor to begin a conversation.
  4. Click on where the blinking cursor is to type a text.
  5. To send a message, press send.
  6. Wait for the doctor to reply.

  1. After a successful medical session with the doctor, a medical note is made available to you.
  2. The medical note contains the doctor’s name, date, prescription and diagnosis.
  3. It is accessible and downloadable from your side drawer.
  • To access the side drawer, press the three lines at the top of the screen.
  1. Navigate to the section labelled medical notes and click on it.
  2. A list of medical notes will appear, sorted by the latest date.
  3. Click on the download button of the one you want to access.
  4. This file will be locally available to you from your phone in pdf format and can be opened and printed if need be.

  1. After successful submission of your personal information, you are directed here.
  2. This screen is to set the days you will be available to offer medical service on the application.
  3. Select the day i.e. Monday, then below it select the times you will be available depending on your flexibility i.e. 1000hrs,1300hrs etc.
  4. You can only select one day at a time, but multiple times are allowed.
  5. Go on and update times for all the days you will be available.
  6. Press the save button to proceed to the next step.

  1. After getting a comprehensive detail about the patient’s condition, you can diagnose and prescribe medication to them.
  2. To write a diagnosis and prescription note; press the paper clip icon.
  3. Type in the diagnosis and prescription in the fields provided.
  4. Press save to make it available to the patient. This marks the end of a medical session.
